About

As a sole proprietorship, Buck a Buck Lawn Service is owned and operated by Duane Joseph, a dedicated professional with a passion for delivering exceptional lawn care services. With Duane at the helm, you can trust that your lawn will receive personalized attention and meticulous care.


As a small business, we prioritize customer satisfaction and strive to build lasting relationships with our clients. You can expect open communication, attention to detail, and a commitment to exceeding your expectations with every service we provide.

Services

Lawn Mowing

Keep your lawn looking pristine with our professional lawn mowing service. Our skilled team will meticulously mow your lawn to ensure a neat and uniform appearance. With attention to detail and precision, we'll leave your lawn looking beautifully manicured and ready to enjoy.

Lawn Care

Elevate the health and beauty of your lawn with our comprehensive lawn care service. We'll customize a plan to address your lawn's specific needs, promoting lush growth and vibrant greenery.
